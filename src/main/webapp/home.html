Who Is Any Given Room?

Any Given Room is an indie rock? pop rock? alternative....? whatever, its all made up and nothing matters, band comprised of 5 trash pandas and 1 actual brick - you guess who is who - based out of Little Rock, Arkansas. Since its creation in 2017 it has grown and changed into the 6-piece dumpster fire that occasionally graces local stages to a solid audience of at least 8 people. The band's sound has morphed from literally just screaming into a metal trashcan to a loose association of sounds in the vague direction of music they describe as "Good-Ass Pop Music." Any Given Room is for fans of Jesus, Stitch from the 2002 Disney Blockbuster "Lilo and Stitch," vaguely threatening dissociative thoughts that lurk in the quiet places of your mind, and Fergie. The band includes Dylan Bradley (vocals), Christian Whitehead (drums), Dustin Renfro (bass), Tyler Merriott (guitar), Jeremy Gifford (guitar) and Stephen Sivils (the talent).
