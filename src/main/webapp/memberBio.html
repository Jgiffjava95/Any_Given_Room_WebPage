Meet Any Given Room

Dylan Bradley

Dylan Bradley is the vocalist for Any Given Room. His fiery yet catchy vocal melodies come from his background in Metal, Pop, and Gospel. Dylan is always striving to push himself and his performances with each new endeavor.

Steven Sivils

Stephen is the keyboard player of Any Given Room. His Various, Dynamic synthesizer and electric piano sounds act as the "sugar and spice" for the music. He is strongly influenced by EDM, Pop, and even a few Metal bands.

Christian Whitehead

Christian is the drummer of Any Given Room. His Hard Rock/Pop infused drumming acts as the rhythmic backbone for the music. He draws inspiration from Metal and Pop music.

Dustin Renfro

Dustin is the bassist of Any Given Room. His playing fused with Christians drumming sets a solid foundation for the rest of the band. His musical backgrounds are in Blues, Rock, Funk Rock, Psychedelia, with strong emphasis on groove.

Jeremy Gifford

Jeremy plays guitar for Any Given Room. His lead and rhythm guitar work pushes and pulls with Tyler's to create the "Math Rock" element that lingers in Any Given Room's music. His inspiration consists of Alternative, Progressive Rock and Metalcore.
